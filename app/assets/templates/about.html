Our Story

Spoiler Alert: We make fearsome audio electronics.

Swan Audio has been around in the frostbitten audio wastelands of Atlantic Canada since 2012. As technology enthusiasts, seeing the lack of relative innovation in audio in comparison to other fields left us confused. The company started as a side project to find answers to why the 'status-quo' was the way it was.

Talking about the future is weird in an industry where everyone is trying to relive the past. Our future is about enabling you to create the kind of sounds that you hear in your head. The sounds that comes as a result from knowing what you want.

The future awaits.

Our Ideals

  • Enjoy the Ride

    We stress test our products, not ourselves. If something isn't fun to work on, it's time to work on something else.

  • Drive Change

    This music industry is filled with vintage technology. Lets bring things into the 21st century.

  • Build with Heart

    A lot of pride into everything we make. If it's not good enough for us, then it's not good enough for anyone.

  • Empower the Individual

    You need knowledge to make informed choices. We believe in passing on what we know, to allow you to sculpt the sounds you hear in your head.

  • No Bullshit

    Reality is root level for us. If something can't be demonstrated or measured, it does not exist.
